THE XCOM INITIATIVE

The increased alien attacks, and the complete lack of a viable respone from any one nation, have proven conclusively that the alien threat cannot be dealt with without a multinational effort. It is therefore the conclusion of this council that the XCOM initative should be reinstated, effective immediately

Should this proposal be ratified each member nation will have to commit its resources, both in terms of money and personal, in order to create a fighting force truly capable of fending off the invading force. This contribution will be proportional to the GDP of each nation. The project will cease to protect any nation that withdraws its funding.

The XCOM initative offers the following exciting features:

A chance to discover the nature of the invaders, and to buy their corpses in bulk. Why would you buy a truckload of sectoid corpses? Why not?

The brave men and women of the XCOM initative go through intensive training, making them the fiercest warriors on earth. Their training includes:

  • Eight months of grenade throwing drills
  • Psychological exercises that make them fit to face the alien threat without panicking
  • Teamwork and trust exercises, causing them to almost never shot their teammates when panicking
  • Shooting ranges, probably

The ability to protect your nation and obtain advanced alien technology, while the poorer nations of the world are completely helpless.
