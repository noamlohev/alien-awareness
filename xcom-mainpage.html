THE XCOM INITIATIVE

The increased alien attacks, and the complete lack of a viable respone from any one nation, have proven conclusively that the alien threat cannot be dealt with without a multinational effort. It is therefore the conclusion of this council that the XCOM initative should be reinstated, effective immediately

Should this proposal be ratified each member nation will have to commit its resources, both in terms of money and personal, in order to create a fighting force truly capable of fending off the invading force. This contribution will be proportional to the GDP of each nation. The project will cease to protect any nation that withdraws its funding.
